Our Process
From first call to live campaigns in 7 days. Here's exactly what happens when you work with Max Marketing.
1
Free Strategy Call
We start with a free 30-minute call to understand your business, your goals, and your ideal customer. No pitch, no pressure — just a real conversation about what growth looks like for you.
We'll cover your service area, average job value, current lead sources, and what platforms make the most sense for your business. You'll leave with a clear picture of exactly what we'd run for you.
30 minutes · Free · No obligation
2
Onboarding & Account Setup
Once you're onboard, we send you a short questionnaire covering your business details, target customers, and goals. We then set up your ad accounts and connect your billing directly to Google and Meta.
Your card connects directly to the platforms — we never touch your ad spend. We also install conversion tracking on your website so every call, form, and booking is measured from day one.
24 hours · Questionnaire takes ~10 minutes
3
We Build Your Campaigns
Our team builds your full campaign — keyword research, ad copy, audience targeting, creative direction, and campaign structure. Everything is tailored to your specific business, service area, and offer.
We write multiple ad variations for testing, set up proper campaign structure for maximum quality score, and prepare all creatives before submitting for your review.
3–5 days to build
4
You Review & Approve Everything
Nothing goes live without your sign-off. We send you every ad, every headline, and every creative for review before launch. You have full visibility and control over what represents your business.
Most clients approve within 1–2 business days. We incorporate any feedback and confirm everything meets your standards before we hit go.
Your approval required · Full transparency
5
Launch & Daily Optimization
Campaigns go live and we monitor performance daily. We continuously optimize — pausing underperforming ads, scaling winners, refining targeting, and adjusting bids to drive down your cost per lead.
The first 30 days are critical for gathering data. We make frequent adjustments in this period to find what works best for your specific market and audience.
Daily monitoring · Continuous optimization
6
Monthly Performance Report
Every month you receive a clear, jargon-free report showing your total leads, cost per lead, return on ad spend, and a breakdown of what drove results. We also outline our plan for the following month.
You'll always know exactly where your ad spend went, what it produced, and what we're doing next to improve results.
Monthly · Full cost & results breakdown

FAQ
Common Questions
Who pays for the actual ads? +
You pay Google and Meta directly for your ad spend. We never handle your advertising budget. Your card connects directly to the platforms and you see every charge. Our fee is separate and covers strategy, management, and reporting only.
Do you guarantee results? +
We don't guarantee specific numbers — no honest agency does. What we guarantee is professional campaign management, continuous optimization, and full transparency. Our average clients see a 2–4x return on ad spend, but results vary by market, budget, and industry.
How long until I see results? +
Most clients start seeing leads within the first week of launch. Google Search ads can generate calls within hours of going live. The first 30 days are an optimization period where we gather data and improve performance. Results typically strengthen significantly in months 2 and 3.
How much should I budget for ad spend? +
We recommend a minimum of $500–$1,000/month in ad spend on top of our management fee. This gives us enough data to optimize effectively and generate meaningful lead volume. More ad spend generally means more leads, as long as campaigns are well managed.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. We require 30 days written notice to cancel. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation penalties. We earn your business every month through results — not through locking you in.
Will I see the ads before they go live? +
Absolutely. Nothing goes live without your approval. We build every ad, headline, and creative and send it to you for review first. You have full visibility and control over everything that represents your business.
Do I need a website? +
Yes — a website is required to run the most effective campaigns. We use your website to set up conversion tracking, install the Meta Pixel, and send ad traffic to a dedicated landing page. If your website needs improvement we can advise on what changes will most impact campaign performance.
What makes Max Marketing different from other agencies? +
We specialize exclusively in home service businesses — HVAC, plumbing, roofing, and electrical. We don't take on every type of client. This focus means we understand your industry, your customers, and what makes them convert. We're also fully transparent — your ad spend goes directly to the platforms, you approve every ad before launch, and you own all your accounts.
