Client Onboarding

Let's get your
campaigns live.

Follow the steps below to connect your ad accounts. The whole process takes about 10 minutes per platform and you stay in full control the entire time.

Your billing is 100% secure. Your card connects directly to Google and Meta — we have zero visibility into your payment details and zero ability to charge or withdraw funds. You can monitor every cent of ad spend in real time through your own dashboard.
Step 1 of 2
Google Ads Setup
Connect your Google Ads account so we can build and manage your search campaigns.
1
Sign in to Google Ads
Go to ads.google.com and sign in with your Google account. If you don't have one, create a free Google account first.
2
Switch to Expert Mode
If you see a campaign creation screen, look for "Switch to Expert Mode" or "Change View" at the bottom or top of the page. Click it. This bypasses Google's simplified Smart Mode and gives you the full account.
Important — don't skip this step
3
Create account without a campaign
Click the small link that says "Create an account without a campaign." This lets you set up the account first before any ads run.
4
Enter your business info
Confirm your business name, time zone, and currency. Click Submit.
5
Add your billing
Click "Explore Your Account", then go to Tools & Settings (wrench icon) → Billing → Settings. Add your credit or debit card. Google charges this card directly — Max Marketing cannot see these details.
Your card is encrypted and stored securely by Google. Max Marketing is added as a campaign manager only — we have no access to billing.
6
Set your daily budget
When prompted for a budget, divide your monthly ad spend by 30 to get your daily amount. For example, $1,000/month = $33/day. We will fine-tune this once your campaigns are live.
Example: $1,000/mo ÷ 30 = $33/day
7
Go to Access and Security
In the left sidebar click Admin, then click Access and Security.
8
Add Max Marketing as a user
Click the blue + button and enter our email:
maxmarketingofficial2026@gmail.com
9
Grant admin access and send invite
Set the access level to Admin and click Send Invitation. We will accept the invite and your Google Ads setup is complete. Campaigns can then be built and launched.
Setup complete — we'll take it from here
Step 2 of 2
Meta Ads Setup
Connect your Meta Business Manager so we can run campaigns on Facebook and Instagram.
1
Create your Meta Business Manager
Go to business.facebook.com/overview and click Create Account.
2
Enter your business details
Enter your business name, your name, and your business email. Check your email for a verification link from Meta and click it before continuing. Check your spam folder if you don't see it within a few minutes.
3
Add your payment method
Go to Business Settings (gear icon) → Payment Methods. Add your credit or debit card. Meta charges this card when ads run — Max Marketing cannot see these details.
Your card is encrypted and stored securely by Meta. Max Marketing has no access to your billing information.
4
Set your daily budget
When we build your campaigns, we will set your daily budget based on your monthly ad spend divided by 30. For example, $1,000/month = $33/day. You will approve this before anything goes live.
Example: $1,000/mo ÷ 30 = $33/day
5
Add Max Marketing as a user
Under the Users tab in the left sidebar, click People, then click Add.
6
Invite Max Marketing
Enter our email, toggle on Full Control (Admin), and click Next:
max@maxmarketingagency.com
7
Assign your ad account
In the pop-up window, select Ad Accounts from the left column. Check the box for your specific ad account. Toggle on Manage Campaigns or Full Control.
8
Send the invite
Click Invite. We will accept and can now build and manage your Facebook and Instagram campaigns.
Setup complete — we'll take it from here
